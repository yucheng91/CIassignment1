The Walters

The Walters were an unsigned five-piece indie rock band from Chicago, Illinois. As of 2017 they had released two albums and three singles and were touring the United States on their first national tour.

The band formed in Chicago Illinois in 2014 after the band's guitarist and namesake Walter Kosner moved from Connecticut to Chicago in an attempt to attend DePaul University. After dropping out of the school, Kosner recruited the rest of the band members who eventually would form The Walters. That same year the band released their first recorded effort, Songs for Dads. The release saw the band charting on Spotify's United States Viral Top 50. This caught the attention of Mario Cuomo,singer of The Orwells, who brought the band's music to Canvasback Records.
